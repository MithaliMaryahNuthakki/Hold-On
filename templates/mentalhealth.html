EXPLORATORY ANALYSIS ON MENTAL HEALTH

This is an analysis to see how the world compares in a variety of categories related to one's MENTAL HEALTH.

This is the result of the analysis performed on the data set, "survey.csv(kaggle)".
We must note that the folowing analysis is completely based on the survey alone and the actuality might vary.

AGEWISE COUNT

Reviewing the above garph we can see that people between the age group of 30-40 are most likely to be affected where as the age group 20-30 is the next highest with not much of difference.


MEN VS WOMEN COUNT

According to this data set men suffer more than women, but we have to keep in mind all the plots are only from the dataset provided and the actuality may vary.


INTERFERENCE IN WORK/SCHOOL

From the above graph we can infer that having a mental disorder afftects people in their work or school life sometimes.

AVAILING TREATMENT

We can see that about 50% people dont avail any treatment, there can be multiple reasons like financial problem but the majority of the people avoid treatment due societal pressures.

PRESENCE OF FAMILY HISTORY

Mental Disorder maybe present without any history in the family,about 61% of the people admitted had no history in the family. Anyone, anywhere can suffer from it.

WORKING IN TECH

Majority of the people working int the tech industry suffer the most about 82%. This is the highest over any individual profession, this may be due to the competetivity of the industry and fear of loosing out.
