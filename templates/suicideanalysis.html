EXPLORATORY ANALYSIS ON SUICIDE RATES

This is an analysis of people commiting suicide all over India.

This is the result of the analysis performed on the data set, "survey.csv(kaggle)".
We must note that the folowing analysis is completely based on the survey alone and the actuality might vary.

"Click on Each graph to zoom"

OVER THE YEARS

Reviewing the above garph we can see that suicide percentage is increasing over the years.


TOP 10 CAUSES

According to this data set we can say that family problems are the major causes of suicide.


TOP 10 STATES

From the above graph we can infer that in India maximum number suicide happens in Maharashtra and second maximum number occurs in West Bengal.

FEMALE VS MALE

We can see that Men kill themselves than women. But we should also be aware that these are only from dataset and the reality may have some changes.

EDUCATION STATUS

By the above graph we can see that suicide percentage decreases with higher level of education. Also educated people are more aware than people with less or no education.

MARITAL STATUS

Majority of suicide is committed by married people. Also second majority is the people who are never married.
